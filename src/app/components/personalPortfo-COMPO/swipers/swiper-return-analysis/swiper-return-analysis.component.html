What is Composite Index?

What is Composite Index?

As every portfolio is unique, we create a customized benchmark index basis your portfolio allocation to Large, Mid and Small/Micro cap stocks. Basis your allocations, we virtually invest your investment amount in BSE Sensex, BSE Midcap and BSE Small cap and then track the investments in these indices. The purpose of creating a composite index is so that you always have a right benchmark to compare your returns with.
