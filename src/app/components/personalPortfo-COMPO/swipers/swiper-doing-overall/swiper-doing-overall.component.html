7 Dot Portfolio Analysis

  • Returns
    • Your portfolio returns since you created your portfolio are compared to returns generated by your composite index during the same period
    • Colour of the dot is dependent on the relative performance of your portfolio and composite index
  • Risk
    • Your portfolio volatility (standard deviation) since you created your portfolio is compared to volatility of your composite index during the same period
    • Colour of the dot is dependent on the relative performance of your portfolio and composite index
  • Liquidity
    • Proportion of your portfolio that may consist of stocks with liquidity issue
    • Liquidity issue may be if
      • Quantity held is more than 2% of the stock’s 5-day average volume or,
      • Quantity held is more than 5% of the stock’s 5-day average delivery volume or,
      • Stocks that may take more than 1 day to liquidate (basis 2% of 5-day average volume) or,
    • While selling stocks with liquidity issues, you may face longer time or you may not get exit at your desired price Diversification
  • Diversification
    • Diversification is assessed basis the construct of your portfolio
    • We not only look at the number of stocks but also your allocation to Sectors, Market Caps and holdings
    • We exclude tail stocks while studying number of stocks; tail stocks are the ones with less than 1% allocation in portfolio
    • We penalize under as well as over diversification
  • Quality
    • Construct of your portfolio and Quality of your portfolio stocks as per Markets Mojo’s Quality assessment module
    • Quality signifies long term financial performance of a company vis-à-vis its competitors in the industry. We believe high quality companies with consistent record of financial performance will reward investors in the long run.
  • Valuation
    • Construct of your portfolio and Valuation of your portfolio stocks as per Markets Mojo’s Valuation assessment module
    • Valuation refers to attractiveness of the company at its current stock price by looking at its past financial performance and valuation matrices. We believe valuation helps the investor to estimate the timing of entry and exit in stocks.
  • Financial Trend
    • Construct of your portfolio and Financial Trend of your portfolio stocks as per Markets Mojo’s Financial Trend assessment module
    • Financial trend analyses result of the company in the recent quarters. We believe companies which have been trending higher on financial trend in the latest periods have potential to generate returns in the short term.
