Elements on the Price Movement Card

  • Price Chart
    • The interactive chart gives a quick view of the movement in Portfolio Value during the selected time frame
    • The Portfolio Value is adjusted for any buying/selling activity done by you, because of which there wonâ€™t be spikes at the time of your transactions
    • The graph is updated every 15 minutes
    • The Green area highlights the periods where the portfolio is above its previous close and the Red area highlights the periods where the portfolio is below its previous close
  • Headline
    • Basis the period selected this starts the money made during the period and performance as compared to broad market (Sensex) during the period
    • Portfolio returns consider not just returns made yon our current investments but also on your past investments as well as the dividends received
  • Returns
    • The portfolio return in the period is compared with your customized composite index and sensex in the period
    • Portfolio returns consider not just returns made yon our current investments but also on your past investments as well as the dividends received
    • The returns do not include the tax impact on securities sold or the notional tax that you may pay if you liquidate your holdings today
  • Composite Index
    • As every portfolio is unique, we create a customized benchmark index basis your portfolio allocation to Large, Mid and Small/Micro cap stocks
    • Basis your allocations, we virtually invest your investment amount in BSE Sensex, BSE Midcap and BSE Small cap and then track the investments in these indices
    • The purpose of creating a composite index is so that you always have a right benchmark to compare your returns with
  • Top Contributor and Drag
    • Basis the performance of your portfolio stocks today, the stock that made and lost the most money for you today are categorized as Top Contributor and Top Drag respectively
  • Advance and Decline
    • The number of days in the selected period where the portfolio ended with a profit or loss are categorized as advance and decline respectively

You can click on the time period tabs and see the analysis for the selected period
